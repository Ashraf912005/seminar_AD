Our Services

Behavioral Anomalies

Deviations in behavior such as odd login times or abnormal activity. Detected using rule-based and machine learning methods. Prevent with MFA, monitoring, and training.

Salary Anomalies

Payments outside normal ranges or sudden bonuses. Detected via rules and ML on payroll data. Prevent with approval workflows, audits, and benchmarks.

Transaction Anomalies

Large or frequent irregular transactions. Detected using thresholds and neural networks. Prevent using limits, verification, and real-time systems.

Time-Based Anomalies

Access or actions at odd hours (e.g., 1 AM–5 AM). Use time rules and ML temporal analysis. Prevent with restricted hours and alerts.

Geo/IP Anomalies

Logins from unknown countries/IPs. Use geofencing, whitelisting, and login alerts. Detect using IP rules and geo-clustering.
